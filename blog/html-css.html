What are the best practices associated with using classes vs ids?

What Are Classes and IDs?

Let's say that you are decorating a house. You have different rooms and you want to style them in various ways.

-Classes are like general decorations i.e. kitchen-style decoration or bedroom-style decoration

-IDs are like unique decorations. These are one of a kind items designated specifically for a certain room i.e. waterproof-bathroom-painting

When to Use Each?

Classes are used when you want to apply the same style to several rooms. For example if you want beige colored curtains in all rooms, you would apply the "room-curtain" style to all bedroom windows.

IDs are used for items that are unique and appears only once, like a special portrait of your dog displayed in the kitchen. It is not ideal to use ID for everything as your room becomes too specific -making things complicated when you want to apply similar styles to multiple rooms
